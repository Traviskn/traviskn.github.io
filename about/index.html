Hi there! Thanks for checking out my blog.

My Background

I grew up near Seattle, Washington and went to school at Brigham Young University studying Information Systems. Now I live in Salt Lake Utah with my wife and daughter.

I spent two years living in Japan serving as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I lived and worked in Shikoku, Wakayama, Okayama, Osaka, and Kobe. I love the people and culture of Japan!

I am passionate about technology and love building web and mobile applications. I am experienced with both front and back end development. Some technologies I work with include React.js, React Native, Node.js, Ruby on Rails, Django, PostgreSQL, and RethinkDB.
